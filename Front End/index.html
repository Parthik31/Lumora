The Solar Adoption Challenges We Solve

Despite the benefits of solar energy, many Indian households face significant barriers to adoption:

High Upfront Costs

The initial investment for solar panels and installation is prohibitive for many families, with unclear payback periods.

Battery Storage Issues

Affordable battery solutions for storing solar energy during nights or cloudy days are hard to find and understand.

Limited Rooftop Space

Urban homes often don't have enough terrace space for optimal solar panel installation.

Information Gap

Most people don't understand how solar works, potential savings, or how to claim available subsidies.
