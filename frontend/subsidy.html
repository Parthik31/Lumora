Solar Subsidy Guidance

Step-by-step assistance to claim government subsidies for your solar installation

1

Check Eligibility

Determine if you qualify for solar subsidies under the PM Surya Ghar Yojana or state-specific schemes:

  • Residential consumers with valid electricity connection
  • Property ownership documents
  • Rooftop space availability (minimum 100 sq ft for 1kW system)
  • No existing solar installation at the property
2

Required Documents

Prepare these documents for your subsidy application:

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar card)
  • Address proof
  • Latest electricity bill
  • Property ownership documents
  • Bank account details (for subsidy disbursement)
  • Technical feasibility report from DISCOM
3

Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for subsidy:

  1. Register on the National Portal for Rooftop Solar
  2. Apply for feasibility approval from your local DISCOM
  3. Select an empaneled vendor from the portal
  4. Get installation done by the selected vendor
  5. Submit installation details and apply for subsidy
  6. Wait for inspection and subsidy approval
4

Subsidy Amount

Current subsidy structure under PM Surya Ghar Yojana:

System Capacity Central Financial Assistance (CFA)
Up to 3kW ₹18,000 per kW
Above 3kW to 10kW ₹18,000 per kW for first 3kW, ₹9,000 per kW thereafter
Above 10kW ₹1,17,000 fixed

Additional state subsidies may be available in some regions.

5

Tracking & Disbursement

After application submission:

  • Track your application status on the national portal
  • Subsidy amount is typically disbursed within 30 days of inspection
  • Amount is credited directly to your bank account
  • Contact DISCOM helpline for any delays or issues
